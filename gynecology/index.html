Common Symptoms We Treat

If you or your family members are experiencing any of the following symptoms, scheduling a medical consultation at Hermes Health Care in Ullal is the first step toward diagnosis and relief. Ignoring early warning signs can lead to complications and chronic health challenges.

Irregular Menstrual Cycles

Periods that are consistently early, late, skip months, or present unusually heavy or painful bleeding.

Severe Pelvic Pain & Cramps

Intense pain in the lower abdomen before or during periods, or during pelvic movement, indicating endometriosis/PCOS.

Difficulty Conceiving (Infertility)

Inability to achieve pregnancy after 12 months of unprotected intercourse, requiring reproductive screenings.

Abnormal Vaginal Discharge

Discharge that is green, yellow, thick white, or has an unusual odor, pointing to pelvic or yeast infections.

Hormonal Fluctuations & Acne

Excessive facial hair growth, persistent adult acne, or unexplained weight gain, indicating PCOS.

Hot Flashes & Mood Disturbances

Sudden warm sensations, night sweats, and sleep disturbances in women over 45, indicating perimenopause.

Our Diagnostic Approach

Accurate treatment depends entirely on precise diagnostics. At Hermes Health Care in Ullal, Bengaluru, we employ a meticulous diagnostic approach. Our in-house clinical laboratory operates under strict calibration standards, utilizing modern diagnostic equipment to deliver highly reliable results.
