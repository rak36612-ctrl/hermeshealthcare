Common Symptoms We Treat

If you or your family members are experiencing any of the following symptoms, scheduling a medical consultation at Hermes Health Care in Ullal is the first step toward diagnosis and relief. Ignoring early warning signs can lead to complications and chronic health challenges.

Gradual or Sudden Vision Blur

Difficulty reading, driving, or recognizing faces due to loss of vision clarity or focus.

Persistent Redness & Eye Pain

Bloodshot eyes, throbbing pain, or foreign body sensations, indicating corneal injury or infection.

Excessive Tearing or Dry Sensation

Eyes that are constantly dry, gritty, or watering excessively due to tear duct imbalances.

Frequent Headaches & Eye Strain

Aches around the eyes or forehead after reading or screen work, suggesting refractive errors.

Sensitivity to Light (Photophobia)

Discomfort or pain in the eyes when exposed to normal daylight or indoor lighting.

Halos Around Lights & Night Blindness

Seeing rings around headlights at night or experiencing difficulty seeing in low-light environments.
