Common Symptoms We Treat

If you or your family members are experiencing any of the following symptoms, scheduling a medical consultation at Hermes Health Care in Ullal is the first step toward diagnosis and relief. Ignoring early warning signs can lead to complications and chronic health challenges.

Persistent High Fever

High body temperature accompanied by chills, body aches, and fatigue that lasts more than 48 hours.

Chronic Cough & Congestion

Prolonged respiratory symptoms, dry or productive cough, sore throat, or mild breathing difficulty.

Unexplained Weight Loss

Sudden or progressive weight loss without modifications to dietary habits or physical activity levels.

Persistent Fatigue & Weakness

Ongoing exhaustion that does not resolve with rest, pointing to potential metabolic or system disorders.

Gastric Discomfort & Nausea

Frequent bloating, abdominal cramps, acid reflux, vomiting, or changes in normal bowel patterns.

Migraines & Severe Headaches

Recurrent, throbbing head pain that interferes with daily tasks and is often accompanied by light sensitivity.

Our Diagnostic Approach

Accurate treatment depends entirely on precise diagnostics. At Hermes Health Care in Ullal, Bengaluru, we employ a meticulous diagnostic approach. Our in-house clinical laboratory operates under strict calibration standards, utilizing modern diagnostic equipment to deliver highly reliable results.
