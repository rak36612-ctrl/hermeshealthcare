Common Symptoms We Treat

If you or your family members are experiencing any of the following symptoms, scheduling a medical consultation at Hermes Health Care in Ullal is the first step toward diagnosis and relief. Ignoring early warning signs can lead to complications and chronic health challenges.

Persistent Acid Reflux (GERD)

Frequent burning sensation in the chest (heartburn) and acid regurgitation after meals.

Chronic Bloating & Flatulence

Uncomfortable abdominal fullness and excessive gas that does not resolve with simple dietary modifications.

Ongoing Abdominal Pain & Cramps

Recurrent abdominal discomfort, especially before or after meals, suggesting digestive inflammation.

Frequent Loose Stools or Diarrhea

Bowel movements that are watery, loose, and occur more than three times daily for over a week.

Severe Chronic Constipation

Difficulty passing stools, infrequent bowel movements (less than three times a week), and straining.

Unexplained Jaundice or Nausea

Yellowing of the eyes or skin, accompanied by dark urine, pointing to potential liver or gallbladder dysfunction.
