An ophthalmologist is a medical eye specialist who diagnoses and treats all eye conditions, performs vision testing, prescribes glasses/lenses, treats infections, and screens for diseases like cataracts and glaucoma.

Glaucoma is detected by measuring intraocular pressure (tonometry), examining the optic nerve, and checking peripheral visual field ranges during an eye exam.
