Daycare surgeries are minor procedures that do not require overnight hospitalization, such as sebaceous cyst excision, lipoma removal, abscess drainage, minor wound debridement, and diabetic foot management. Patients are discharged on the same day.

You should consult a general surgeon for swellings (cysts, lipomas), non-healing wounds, diabetic foot ulcers, minor skin infections needing surgical intervention, or for evaluations of abdominal pain/hernias.
