Common Symptoms We Treat

If you or your family members are experiencing any of the following symptoms, scheduling a medical consultation at Hermes Health Care in Ullal is the first step toward diagnosis and relief. Ignoring early warning signs can lead to complications and chronic health challenges.

Frequent Urination (Polyuria)

Increased frequency of urination, particularly during night hours, due to high blood glucose overload.

Excessive Thirst (Polydipsia)

An unquenchable sensation of thirst, even after consuming large volumes of fluids throughout the day.

Unusual Extreme Hunger

Increased appetite (polyphagia) as cells remain starved of glucose due to insufficient insulin action.

Slow-Healing Wounds & Cuts

Minor cuts, scratches, or bruises that take an unusually long time to heal or develop minor infections.

Blurred Vision & Dizziness

Temporary or progressive vision blurriness caused by fluid shifting in the lenses of the eyes due to high sugar.

Numbness in Hands or Feet

Tingling, burning, or loss of sensation in the extremities, indicating early diabetic neuropathy.
